## 1.0 Executive Summary

This policy provides comprehensive commercial coverage for TechStart Manufacturing's 45,000 sq ft production facility in Austin, TX. Coverage includes property, general liability, product liability, business interruption, and workers' compensation for 87 employees across manufacturing and office operations.

**Key Coverage Highlights:**
- $5M aggregate general liability
- $10M product liability (critical for electronics manufacturing)
- 12-month business interruption coverage
- Cyber liability endorsement included
