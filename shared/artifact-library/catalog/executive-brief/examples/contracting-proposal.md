## 1.0 Executive Summary

Summit Construction Group is pleased to submit this proposal for the comprehensive renovation of the 3rd floor at 450 Commerce Street. This 18,500 sq ft project transforms dated office space into a modern, collaborative workplace featuring open floor plans, upgraded MEP systems, and premium finishes.

**Project Highlights:**
- **Duration:** 14 weeks (January 6 - April 11, 2026)
- **Scope:** Complete gut renovation including demolition, MEP, drywall, flooring, and finishes
- **Approach:** Phased construction to minimize disruption to occupied floors
- **Value Engineering:** $127K in savings identified through alternate materials
